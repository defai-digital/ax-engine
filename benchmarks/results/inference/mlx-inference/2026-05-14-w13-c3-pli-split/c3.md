# W1.3 C3 — per-layer-input split (2026-05-14)

PRD: `MLX-DECODE-SUBMIT-COST-PRD.md` W1.3
Audit: `MLX-DECODE-W13-CAST-RESHAPE-AUDIT.md` C3

## Change

`compute_per_layer_inputs_arr` (model.rs L2102) was issuing 35 separate
`slice` dispatches to extract per-layer chunks of the combined per-layer
input tensor. Replaced with one `mlx_split` along axis 2; each part is
the same strided view as the previous slice produced, so the per-layer
`reshape` to drop the singleton dim is unchanged.

```rust
let parts = split(&combined, num_layers as i32, 2, None);
let per_layer = parts.into_iter()
    .map(|s| reshape(&s, &[1, seq, per_layer_dim as i32], None))
    .collect();
```

## A/B (M5 Max, gemma-4-e2b-it-4bit, 128/128, 3 reps)

| Metric | C1 baseline | C3 | Δ |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| decode_tok_s (median) | 185.23 | 184.96 | -0.15% |
| direct_pipeline_wall_us / step | 5,339.66 | 5,343.51 | +3.85 µs |
| direct_pipeline_async_eval_wall_us / step | 4,917.72 | 4,917.10 | -0.61 µs |
| **direct_pipeline_op_count / step** | **1,803** | **1,769** | **-34** |

Op-count drop matches the audit's prediction exactly (35 slice dispatches
→ 1 split). Throughput delta is within single-rep noise on a 5,340
µs/step total — the 34 × ~2 µs/op savings (~68 µs/step) sits at the noise
floor of a 3-rep median.

The change is Gemma 4 specific (per-layer-input gating only fires when
`hidden_size_per_layer_input != 0`). Other model families take the early
return at the top of `compute_per_layer_inputs_arr` and are unaffected.
